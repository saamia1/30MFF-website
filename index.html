Sustainable Living

Living sustainably at NYUAD means making conscious choices to reduce waste, conserve energy, and protect the environment. Small actions, like turning off unused lights, using refillable bottles, and properly sorting waste, help minimize our ecological footprint. Together, we can create a greener campus and a healthier planet.

Energy Conservation

Saving energy is a crucial step toward sustainability. Be mindful of your electricity usage: turn off appliances when not in use, utilize natural light whenever possible, and take the stairs instead of the elevator when feasible. Every small effort contributes to a more efficient and eco-friendly community at NYUAD.
